PhD Candidate in Electrical Engineering

I work in a Systems Neuroscience group at Washington University with Dr. Barani Raman. My doctoral research involves working with: a) developing robust classification (decoding) models to identify sensory stimuli from neural data. b) developing behavioral prediction models based on the insights learned from neural data. c) study cell-intrinsic and network-level mechanisms that may result in adaptive computations. My expertise includes Neuroscience, Machine Learning and Signal processing.
